About Me

I'm a passionate web developer with a love for video game development, art, and tabletop RPGs. My journey as a developer began when I discovered the amazing world of game development using the Godot engine. Experimenting with GDScript, I fell in love with the process of bringing virtual worlds to life and crafting interactive experiences. Alongside game development, my artistic side flourished as I explored different art styles and mediums to create immersive visuals for my projects.

In addition to game development, I have been training in web development technologies through the Scion Coalition Scheme. Proficient in languages such as C#, Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I can create dynamic and responsive websites that provide seamless user experiences. Whether it's building user interfaces or implementing backend functionality, I enjoy tackling the challenges that come with creating engaging web applications.

When I'm not immersed in coding or designing, you can find me engrossed in tabletop RPGs. I love crafting intricate stories and creating unique characters that come to life in collaborative storytelling adventures. Drawing inspiration from the worlds of RPGs, I bring a creative and imaginative mindset to my development work, constantly exploring new ways to engage users and create memorable experiences.
