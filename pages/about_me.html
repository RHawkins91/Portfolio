About Me

I'm a passionate web developer with a love for video game development, art, and tabletop RPGs.
My journey as a programmer began when I discovered the amazing world of game development using the Unity engine. Experimenting with C#, I fell in love with the process of bringing virtual worlds to life and crafting interactive experiences.
Over the course of my pursuit I learned Python, and from there GDscript, the language used in the free and open source engine, Godot.
Alongside game development, my artistic side flourished as I explored different art styles and mediums to create immersive visuals for my projects.

I have also been training in web development technologies through the Scion Coalition Scheme. Proficient in languages such as C#, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I can create dynamic and responsive websites that provide seamless user experiences.
Whether it's building user interfaces or implementing backend functionality, I enjoy tackling the challenges that come with creating engaging web applications.

When I'm not immersed in coding or designing, you can find me engrossed in tabletop RPGs. I love crafting intricate stories and creating unique characters that come to life in collaborative storytelling adventures.
Drawing inspiration from the worlds of RPGs, I bring a creative and imaginative mindset to my development work, constantly exploring new ways to engage users and create memorable experiences.
